Odoo Shipstation Connector

Gateway to connect Multiple shipping carriers with Your Odoo!

The module Odoo Shipstation Connector facilitates you to integrate multiple shipping carriers like USPS, UPS, DHL, etc to Odoo. It helps you with the efficient processing, fulfilling, and shipping of orders via major shipping carriers.
Odoo, a single solution for all your business related problems; it is widely known for its frontend as well as backend functionalities. Whether you want to manage products, orders or their delivery; Odoo can do it all efficiently!

ShipStation offers you to develop an effective product allocation strategy so your inventory is always optimized, in sync and flowing out the door. It facilitates your customers to Compare real-time rates as well as delivery times for all carriers and automatically select the best carrier for each shipment based on its cost and delivery time.

Do you want to enhance the delivery system of your Odoo?
Now, you can easily integrate your Odoo with Shipstation without any hassle. Odoo Shipstation Connector allows you to integrate several shipping carriers like USPS, UPS, DHL, etc to your Odoo.
Perks of integrating Shipstation to your Odoo
Talking about the shipping strategies; undoubtedly, the best is to ship the products on time and securely as the customers have high expectations and definitely, no store owner would want to turn down a customer's expectations as it directly affects the revenue.
  • The customers can place the order in the usual way.
  • The customers will find the Shipstation Shipping carrier options on the checkout page.
  • The customer can choose any feasible shipping carrier for delivering the order.
  • If you define a handling fee, it will add up with the shipping method on the checkout page.
  • The chosen shipping carrier will appear before confirming the order.
  • The customer will find the shipping label in the Order Information Page.

Detailed Features List

Integrates With Multiple Shipping Carriers to Odoo

  • The extension integrates different shipping carriers like USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, etc to Odoo.

Synchronize Orders On ShipStation

  • The order status will sync automatically to the ShipStation account.

Get Live Shipping Rates At Checkout

  • The shipping rates would be calculated as per the admin's origin address.

Generate Shipment Labels

  • You can manage orders, i.e., view, cancel and hold his orders and can generate the shipping labels.

Tracking Number Visibility

  • The customers can view the tracking number for the order placed in the Order Information page.

Configure A Shipping Title

  • You can set a title for Shipstation Shipping. This title will be visible to the customers in the checkout page

Enable ShipStation from the backend
  • Add API credentials.
    • Load Necessary ShipStation Data
  • Make sure to download necessary data first.
  • This will download Associated Marketplaces, Stores, Carriers, Services, Packages, Customers and Products.
    • Setup Shipstation Delivery Carrier
  • You need to setup default shipstation delivery carrier before moving further, this will enable you to download any associated order with your shipstation account.
    • Download Order Data
  • You can now download any associated order in your ShipStation Account
    • Create Order
  • To get rate select the data and choose the shipstation carrier.
  • The corresponding rate would be shown.
    • Upload Sale Order
  • Note: Before creating the delivery you need to upload this sale order data to shipstation account.
  • Once the data the uploaded you can see the order in ShipStation Account.
  • The necessary data will be shown in ShipStation Data Tab in Odoo Sale Order.
    • Generated shipping label
  • Once sale order is confirmed, you need to create the package for the delivery order.
  • Once package is created, you need to click on the "Get ShipStation Label" button to generate shipping Label.
  • Before validating you can see the status of order in ShipStation Data tab.
  • Once the picking is validated the status of ShipStation Order Status will change to "shipped" from "awaiting_shipment".
    • Download the label
  • You can download the generated label of the shipment from Odoo itself.
